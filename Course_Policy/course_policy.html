Course Policy

Attendance

Attendance is required for the success of this course. Participation is critical for hands-on exercises and lab assignments. Attendance will be taken at the beginning of every class using iClicker. This app uses GPS location support. If you are not in the room, DO NOT try and click here on the app. This is considered academic dishonesty and will be treated as such. You are allowed 2 free class skips. Upon your 3rd class absence (without approved written excuse) your final course grade will be lowered by one letter grade. For every 2 absences after or beyond your 3rd absence your grade will be again lowered by a letter grade.

Missed Assignments

A "Two Strike" policy will be in place for all assignments. Students will be allowed to make up for missed work by using a "missed assignment pass". Each student will only have 2 passes per semester. If a student decides to use their missed assignment pass, they must notify the GTA, or professor electronically BEFORE THE ASSIGNMENT DUE DATE. Once a missed assignment pass is used, the student will only have 3 additional days to complete the assignment. Only in exceptional cases and at the discretion of the instructor, a student would be able to make up missed assignments or exams without the use of a student's pass. Assignments are due by the assigned date. Missed assignments that cannot be made up will be assigned a "0".
